kmos

kMC on steroids: A vigorous attempt to make lattice kinetic Monte Carlo modeling as fast as possible.
kmos is being developed in the context of heterogeneous catalysis but might be of use in other applications as well. kmos wants to enable you to create first-principles kinetic Monte Carlo models faster and with less pain.
Some projects are using kmos already.
